GEO QUIZ — BRAND NOTES
======================

APP IDENTITY
  Full name:    Geo Quiz: Flags, Capitals & Foods
  Short name:   Geo Quiz
  Package:      com.worldgeo.quiz
  Category:     Education / Trivia
  Tone:         Friendly, curious, educational. Not gamified-aggressive.
                Approachable for all ages (8+), smart without being academic.

VISUAL IDENTITY
  Primary color:    Indigo / #4F46E5
  Background:       World map imagery, city skylines, food photography
  Typography:       Nunito (Google Fonts) — rounded, readable, modern
  Icon style:       Minimalist globe or map pin with flag/food accent.
                    Adaptive icon: foreground = icon mark, background = white.
                    Monochrome variant required for Android 13+ themed icons.

KEY MESSAGES (for store copy and ASO)
  1. "195 countries, 5 quiz modes" — breadth
  2. "Offline — no internet required" — reliability, privacy
  3. "Flags + Capital Cities + Local Foods" — unique triple-content angle
  4. "Turkish + English" — bilingual, regional appeal
  5. "No ads, no tracking, no account" — trust, GDPR-friendly

AUDIENCE
  Primary:   Students (12-25), travelers, geography enthusiasts
  Secondary: Trivia game fans, language learners
  Geography: Turkey + English-speaking markets initially

DIFFERENTIATION vs COMPETITORS
  - Includes food-to-country mode (rare in geography quiz apps)
  - Dual-language (TR/EN) from day 1
  - High-quality capital city photos (183 countries)
  - Zero data collection (privacy-first)
  - Works offline (no server dependency)

DO NOT
  - Use clip art or generic emoji-only visuals in store graphics
  - Claim "100% coverage" for flags (13 PNG only; rest emoji — truthful)
  - Promise features not yet implemented (leaderboards, multiplayer, etc.)
  - Use animated GIFs for store screenshots (static preferred for review)
