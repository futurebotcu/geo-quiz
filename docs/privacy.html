Privacy Policy

App: Geo Quiz: Flags, Capitals & Foods
Package: com.worldgeo.quiz
Last updated: February 2026

Summary

Geo Quiz is a fully offline geography quiz application. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. This policy explains exactly what the app does and does not do with your information.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email address, phone number)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, advertising ID, IDFA)
  • Location data (GPS or approximate)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports (no crash reporting SDK is integrated)
  • Survey or feedback data
  • Financial or payment information (no in-app purchases)
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or microphone data

2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores the following data only on your device, using the operating system's standard local storage (SharedPreferences on Android / NSUserDefaults on iOS):

Data TypePurposeLocation
Quiz settings (mode, difficulty, question count, timer, continent filter)Remember your preferences between sessionsDevice local storage
Quiz result history (last 20 results: score, date, mode, duration)Display statistics on the Stats screenDevice local storage
Language preference (English/Turkish/system default)Remember your selected languageDevice local storage

This data never leaves your device. It is not uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third party.

3. Internet and Network Access

The app declares the INTERNET permission on Android. This is required by the Google Fonts library included in the app, which may attempt to load font files if not already cached on the device. No user data is transmitted over this connection. The core quiz functionality (all questions, images, flags, and country data) is fully bundled within the app and works without any internet connection.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services including:

  • Analytics platforms (no Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Advertising networks (no AdMob, Facebook Audience Network, etc.)
  • Crash reporting tools (no Firebase Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
  • Social login providers (no Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In, Facebook, etc.)
  • Payment processors (no Stripe, RevenueCat, etc.)
  • Cloud storage or databases (no Firebase, Supabase, AWS, etc.)

5. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13. Because we do not collect any personal data, this app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent regulations.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All locally stored data (quiz results and settings) can be deleted at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear All Data" option in the app's Settings screen, or
  • Uninstalling the app from your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. For significant changes, we will notify users through an app update. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
